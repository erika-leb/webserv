# Markdown Cheatsheet

## 1. Headings
# H1
## H2
### H3
#### H4
##### H5
###### H6

---

## 2. Emphasis
**Bold text**

*Italic text*

***Bold + Italic***

~~Strikethrough~~

---

## 3. Paragraphs & Line Breaks

Paragraphs are separated by a blank line.

Line break (hard break):  
Add **two spaces at the end of a line**  
or use `<br>`.

Example:
Line one␠␠  
Line two  
Line three<br>Line four

---

## 4. Lists

### Unordered
- Item
- Item
  - Nested item
    - Deeper item

### Ordered
1. First
2. Second
   1. Sub-item
   2. Sub-item

### Task Lists (GFM)
- [ ] Unchecked
- [x] Checked

---

## 5. Links

Inline:
[OpenAI](https://openai.com)

Reference style:
[OpenAI][1]

[1]: https://openai.com

---

## 6. Images

Inline:
![Alt text](path/to/image.png)

Referenced:
![Alt][imgref]

[imgref]: path/to/image.png

---
